Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How far in advance do I need to order?

Please order at least 5 days in advance. For large orders or special events, 1–2 weeks ahead is recommended to guarantee your spot.

How do I place an order?

Orders can be placed by texting 352-942-5351 or via Instagram/Facebook DM (@breezysbaked.goods). Send your details and Bree will get back to you quickly.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! Local delivery is available in the Spring Hill, FL area. Mention your address when you DM and we'll confirm if you're in the delivery zone.

Can I customize flavors and designs?

Absolutely. All boxes are mix & match and cookie cakes can include custom messages and designs. Just let us know what you have in mind!

Do you accommodate allergies?

Please let us know about any allergies when you order. While we do our best to accommodate, all items are baked in the same kitchen.

How do the monthly menus work?

Classic and Premium cookie flavors rotate every month. Follow on Instagram or Facebook to see what's available — new flavors drop at the start of each month.
